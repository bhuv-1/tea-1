INGREDIENTS

  • 1/2 cup water (optional)
  • 1/4 tspn tealeaves
  • 1/2 tspn sugar
  • 1/2 cup milk

STEPS

  1. Take a fryingpan and put it on the stove.
  2. Add water in the pan.
  3. Add tealeaves in the pan.
  4. Add sugar and after sometime add milk in it.
  5. Heat it till it starts boiling and stir it and pour it in the cup.
